Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a baked dough base topped with sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

It is customizable, easy to share, and enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
  6. Slice and serve.
