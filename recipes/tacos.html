Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with tortillas and a variety of fillings.

They are quick to prepare and can be customized with different meats, vegetables, and sauces.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef.
  2. Warm the tortillas.
  3. Add beef to each tortilla.
  4. Top with lettuce, cheese, and tomatoes.
  5. Add sour cream and serve.
