Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and seasonings.

It is hearty, flavorful, and perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef and mix in the tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  6. Bake for 35–40 minutes.
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
